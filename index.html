Premium Drinks

Enjoy the most premium drinks at our bar! Wines, specialty cocktails, craft beers and wines and many other great drinks will satisfy all your tastes. With excellent quality and taste, we offer a delightful experience for the connoisseur's soul.

Our bar not only serves great drinks, but also offers delicious food. The menu is diverse with sandwiches, fresh mannequins and excellent main dishes such as grilled meats and fresh seafood. With excellent quality and taste, we offer a memorable dining experience.
